Since Nov 2019, I am a Data Scientist at Intel.

I was a visiting student of Prof. Song Han’s group at MIT. Beside freezing Boston, I have also lived in beautiful Hangzhou and Vancouver, where I was a member of Dual Degree Program between Zhejiang University and Simon Fraser University.

During my undergrade, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Brian Funt on colour vision, and Prof. Ping Tan on attribute recognition. My current research interests focus on scalable machine learning and design automation.

If you find any research intersts we might share with, feel free to drop me an email. I am always open to potential collaborations.
