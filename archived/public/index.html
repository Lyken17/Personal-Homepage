Biography

Currently, I am a final undergraduate student from Simon Fraser University. Before coming to Simon Fraser University, I spent wonderful two years at Zhejiang University. My research interest mainly focus on machine learning and computer vision. Now I am working with Prof. Brian Funt on using deep learning to handle color vision problems. Before that, I also had one year research experience with Prof. Ping Tan on attribute and 3d visions.
